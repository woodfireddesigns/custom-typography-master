01 — Brand

Brand DNA

Indigo Leather sits in the gap between artisan leathercraft (rustic, western) and fashion handbags (beautiful silhouettes, no maker). Michael has fashion sensibility and makes the bags herself. That combination is rare.

Voice — Use
  • Crafted to Order
  • Made in small batches
  • From the collection
  • Limited ready-to-ship pieces
  • Designer-minded
  • 115 steps. Every bag.
Voice — Avoid
  • Fully custom
  • Design your own bag
  • Request a quote
  • Handmade quality
  • Amazing craftsmanship
  • Artisan (if implies rustic)
Inspiration References
  • 1990s Coach — everyday luxury
  • Polène — sculptural, small-batch
  • A.P.C. — restraint, simplicity
  • Lancel — European leather heritage
  • Ralph Lauren — the customer's world
The Indigo Woman
  • 40–65, professionally established
  • Buys intentionally, dresses timeless
  • Shops Ralph Lauren, Madewell, Eileen Fisher
  • Boutique discoverer, quiet confidence
  • Wants beautiful + functional + distinctive
