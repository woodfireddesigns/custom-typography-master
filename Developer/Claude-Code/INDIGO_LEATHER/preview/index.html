Quality, Made Clear

Glove-tanned hide.
Parachute thread.

Every bag is built from full-grain glove-tanned cowhide, pigskin lining, and bonded nylon thread rated for parachute production. Materials chosen for the kind of use a bag actually gets.

About the Materials
Full-grain glove-tanned cowhide
Sourced for texture, hand, and durability. Chosen hide by hide.
Parachute-grade thread
Bonded nylon used in parachute production. Not decorative stitching.
Pigskin lining
Soft, durable, breathable. Holds its structure over years of use.
Leather interfacing
Structure built from leather, not plastic boning. No shortcuts inside.
