Commissions

Something outside the collection.

For requests that fall outside the defined options — one-of-a-kind pieces, specific materials, special projects — a commission may be the right path.

Most customers find what they’re looking for in the collection. Commissions are for everyone else.

Is a commission right for you?

This IS a commission
  • ✓You need a specific material not in the collection
  • ✓You need dimensions or proportions different from existing pieces
  • ✓You’d like hand tooling or a decorative element on the flap
  • ✓You’re looking for a one-of-a-kind piece as a gift
This is NOT a commission — this is Crafted to Order
  • →You want a bag from the collection in an available color
  • →You want to choose your hardware or strap configuration
  • →You want a bag that ships in 4–5 weeks

What to expect

Timeline
8–14 weeks

Depending on complexity, materials, and current workload.

Pricing
$400–$1,200

Commissions are priced individually based on complexity, materials, and finish. A quote is provided before any deposit is required.

The process

  1. 1 Submit the inquiry form below
  2. 2 I’ll follow up within 5–7 business days to discuss the request
  3. 3 If we move forward, a 50% deposit is required to begin
  4. 4 The remaining 50% is due before shipping
