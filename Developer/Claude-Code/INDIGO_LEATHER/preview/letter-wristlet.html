About this piece

Wristlets exist on a spectrum. Most are too small to be useful or too large to qualify as small. The Letter Wristlet lands exactly where it should: a 6-by-9 envelope that fits a phone, folded cash, a card or two, and nothing else. That constraint is the design.

The hardware is a simple wrist strap loop — the same finish as the hardware on the Juniper and Junie, so the pieces coordinate naturally. The pebble grain leather develops a quiet patina. People report buying a second one before they’ve finished breaking in the first.

The Details

  • Italian pebble grain leather — milled, soft, develops patina with use
  • Full pigskin lining, clean and durable
  • Bonded nylon thread throughout
  • Wrist strap with solid loop hardware — antique brass or nickel
  • Zipper closure with leather pull
  • Interior: two card slots, one flat pocket
  • Fits: phone, cards, folded bills
  • Edges hand-painted and burnished
  • Available in 4 core colors plus seasonal Cobalt and Orchid
  • Made one at a time by a single maker
