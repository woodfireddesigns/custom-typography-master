About Our Studio

Made To Age

The Swedish word Lagom suggests items that are in balance, in moderation: perfect-simple . Each LINDQUIST piece will reflect the work of artists inspired by this ethos. At LINDQUIST we focus on naturally-dyed leather goods made for living, made to last, growing patina with each passing year. LINDQUIST objects are designed to be with you for the long haul, developing character alongside you through a lifetime of use.

Artist Symbols

Each LINDQUIST piece is embossed with a symbol which tells the story of how your piece came to be. The numbers represent the edition of your bag and the letters represent the artists who worked on your piece. Learn more about decoding your bag and about our artists here .
